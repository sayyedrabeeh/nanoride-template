Common Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have about working with us

What is the typical response time?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can call us directly at +1 (310) 555-1234.
Do you offer virtual consultations?
Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual consultations via video call. Our E-Design service is perfect for clients who prefer remote collaboration.
What areas do you serve?
Our primary service area is the Los Angeles metro region and surrounding counties. For larger projects, we're happy to travel nationally and internationally. Virtual design services are available worldwide.
Is there a consultation fee?
Initial consultations are complimentary for projects over $10,000. For smaller projects or design consultations only, we charge $500 for a 2-hour session, which is credited toward your project if you decide to move forward.
