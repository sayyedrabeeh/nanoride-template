Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Woodora

How long does a typical project take?
Most residential projects take 12-24 weeks from initial consultation to final reveal. The timeline varies based on project scope, complexity, and material lead times. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase and keep you updated throughout the process.
What is your minimum project size?
We accept projects starting at $50,000 for full-service interior design. For smaller spaces or consultation-only services, we offer flexible packages. During our initial conversation, we'll discuss your budget and determine the best approach for your needs.
Do you work on projects outside your local area?
Yes! While we're based locally, we take on projects nationwide and internationally. For distance projects, we utilize virtual meetings, detailed documentation, and local contractor partnerships to ensure seamless execution regardless of location.
How involved do I need to be in the process?
We believe the best results come from collaboration. You'll be involved in key decision points like concept approval, material selections, and final walkthrough. However, we handle all the details, coordination, and day-to-day management so you can focus on the exciting parts.
What if I don't like the initial design concept?
Our design process includes multiple rounds of revisions. If the initial concept doesn't resonate, we'll refine and adjust until it's exactly right. We include two rounds of revisions in each phase, with additional revisions available if needed. Your satisfaction is our priority.
Do you provide warranties or guarantees?
We stand behind our work with a comprehensive quality guarantee. All custom millwork and installations include a one-year warranty. We also provide 6-month follow-up support to address any concerns and ensure everything is functioning perfectly.
