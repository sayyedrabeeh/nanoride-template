Our Approach

Design Process

A proven methodology that ensures seamless execution from initial consultation to final reveal

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, needs, lifestyle, and budget. We tour your space, take measurements, and discuss your design preferences and functional requirements.

02

Concept Development

Our team creates detailed design concepts including floor plans, 3D renderings, mood boards, and material palettes. We present multiple options and refine based on your feedback.

03

Design Documentation

We develop comprehensive design documents including technical drawings, specifications, and schedules. All materials, finishes, and furnishings are carefully specified and sourced.

04

Implementation & Installation

Our project managers oversee every aspect of execution, coordinating with contractors, vendors, and artisans. We handle procurement, delivery, and installation to ensure perfection.

05

Final Styling & Reveal

We add the finishing touches with curated accessories, artwork, and styling. After a final walkthrough and quality check, we unveil your transformed space.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single-room design typically takes 6-8 weeks from initial consultation to completion, while full home renovations can take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation phase.
What is your fee structure?
We offer flexible pricing structures including flat fees, hourly rates, and percentage-based fees depending on the project type. Most residential projects start with a flat design fee, with furniture and materials billed separately. We provide detailed proposals outlining all costs before beginning any work.
Do you work with clients' existing furniture?
Absolutely! We love incorporating meaningful pieces and heirlooms into new designs. During our initial consultation, we'll assess your existing furniture and determine what works with the new design. We can also recommend reupholstery or refinishing to update cherished pieces.
Can you work within my budget?
Yes! We work with a wide range of budgets and pride ourselves on creating beautiful spaces at various price points. During consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create a design plan that maximizes value while achieving your vision. We're transparent about costs and always seek your approval before making purchases.
Do you offer virtual design services?
Yes! Our E-Design service is perfect for clients outside our local area or those who prefer a more hands-on approach. You'll receive complete design plans, shopping lists, and implementation guides to execute the design yourself, with email support throughout the process.
What areas do you serve?
Our primary service area includes the greater metropolitan region and surrounding counties. For larger projects, we're happy to travel nationally and internationally. Virtual design services are available worldwide. Contact us to discuss your project location.
